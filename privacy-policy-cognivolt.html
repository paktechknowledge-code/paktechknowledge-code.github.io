Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cognivolt ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Cognivolt does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. The app stores game data (scores, streaks, game settings, cognitive profile data) locally on your device only.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads:

  • Device identifiers — Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location — Based on IP address (not precise GPS)
  • App interactions — Ad views and clicks
  • Device information — Device model, operating system version, screen size
  • Diagnostics — Crash logs, performance data

For more information on how Google uses data, visit: Google Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's Settings > Google > Ads and enabling "Opt out of Ads Personalization".

Consent

When required by law (such as in the European Economic Area), we use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request your consent before serving personalized ads.

Children's Privacy

Cognivolt is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data collected through AdMob is shared with Google for the purpose of serving advertisements. No other third parties receive your data.

Data Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device only. We do not hold any of your data on our servers. When you uninstall Cognivolt, all locally stored data (scores, streaks, game settings, cognitive profile data) is automatically and permanently deleted from your device.

For data collected by Google AdMob (advertising ID, ad interactions, approximate location, and diagnostics), please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and Google's Data & Privacy settings to manage or delete that data.

Data Retention

Game data stored locally on your device persists until you uninstall the app or clear app data. Advertising data collected by Google is subject to Google's retention policies.

Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and advertising services is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.

Your Rights

  • Opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings
  • Reset your Advertising ID at any time
  • Uninstall the app to permanently delete all locally stored data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
