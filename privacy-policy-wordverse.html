Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

WordVerse ("we", "our", or "us") is developed by PakTechKnowledge. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application WordVerse (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Information stored locally on your device:

  • Game progress (streaks, coins, levels completed, statistics)
  • App preferences (sound settings, theme preferences)

This data is stored locally using Android DataStore and is not transmitted to our servers.

Information collected by third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your consent. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP): We use UMP to manage your ad consent preferences in compliance with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • To save your game progress locally on your device
  • To display advertisements (banner, interstitial, and rewarded video ads)
  • To improve the App experience

3. Advertising

WordVerse displays ads through Google AdMob, including:

  • Banner ads on menu and result screens
  • Interstitial ads between Word Connect levels (with cooldown)
  • Rewarded video ads that you can choose to watch for in-game benefits (hints, extra attempts, streak repair)

You can manage your ad preferences through the consent dialog shown when you first open the App, or through the App's settings.

4. Data Retention

Game data is stored locally on your device and persists until you uninstall the App or clear its data. We do not store any personal data on external servers.

5. Children's Privacy

WordVerse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is rated "Everyone" and does not contain age-restricted content.

6. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using Android's secure DataStore. No personal data is transmitted to our servers.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Manage ad personalization preferences via the consent dialog
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the App or clearing app data in device settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device's ad settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
