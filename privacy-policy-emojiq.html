Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how EmojiQ ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

EmojiQ does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information. The app stores game data (coins, puzzle progress, streaks, daily challenge status, and settings) locally on your device only.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements, including banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded ads. AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads:

  • Device identifiers — Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location — Based on IP address (not precise GPS)
  • App interactions — Ad views and clicks
  • Device information — Device model, operating system version, screen size
  • Diagnostics — Crash logs, performance data

For more information on how Google uses data, visit: Google Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your device's Settings > Google > Ads and enabling "Opt out of Ads Personalization".

Consent

When required by law (such as in the European Economic Area), we use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request your consent before serving personalized ads. You may update your consent preferences at any time through the consent form presented in the app.

Children's Privacy

EmojiQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data collected through AdMob is shared with Google for the purpose of serving advertisements. No other third parties receive your data.

Data Retention

Game data stored locally on your device persists until you uninstall the app or clear app data. Advertising data collected by Google is subject to Google's retention policies.

Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and advertising services is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings
  • Reset your Advertising ID at any time
  • Request deletion of any data associated with your device by contacting us
  • Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
